Why me?
Based on my own experience, most interviewed candidates are either good with styling and fall short at problem solving, or viceversa. Frontend developers that are also good with algorithms are much more rare to be found than their backend counterparts. Memoizing, time complexity and efficiency is not needed on the UI, until it is. This makes the difference between a laggy application and one that gives users the best experience even under the worst conditions.
